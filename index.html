Before Your Visit

How to Prepare

  • Hair should be at least 1/4 inch long (the length of a grain of rice)
  • Exfoliate 2–3 times per week and moisturize daily between appointments
  • If transitioning from shaving, wait 14–21 days before your first session
  • On the day of your appointment, skip heavy oils, lotions, makeup, and deodorant on the treatment area
  • Do not exfoliate on the day of service

Common Questions

FAQ

How often should I come in?
We recommend every 4–6 weeks for best results. Regular sugaring leads to finer, sparser regrowth over time.
Can I get sugared during pregnancy?
Yes! Sugaring is safe during pregnancy. We just ask that you let your esthetician know so they can adjust the treatment accordingly.
Is sugaring less painful than waxing?
Most clients find sugaring gentler than traditional waxing. The paste is applied at body temperature and removes hair in the natural direction of growth, reducing discomfort.
What if my hair is too short?
If your hair is shorter than 1/4 inch, we may need to reschedule to ensure the best results. This is the minimum length for the sugar paste to grip effectively.
